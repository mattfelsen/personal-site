Employee Vacation Scheduler

A tool to provide transparency to employees of the remaining availability for vacation days. Administrators could set thresholds by day of the week or specific dates per job role and manage availability as requests for days off are received. In addition, blackout dates could be specified to remove availability across all roles.

Created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and SQL.
