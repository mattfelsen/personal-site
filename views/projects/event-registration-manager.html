Event Registration Manager

A portal to allow for discovery, signup, administration, and reporting of various internal training workshops with different access levels according to job role (employees, managers, administrators). Support for participant approval/rejection, marking attendance by workshop facilitators, training credit system, flexible and comprehensive reporting, and more.

Created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and SQL.
