GIF Wall

In addition to traditional headshots, the 2013 thesis students from the Parsons MFA Design+Technology program created animated GIF portraits for the exhibition website. This installation was created to bring this element of the website into the exhibition gallery itself and was displayed on a wall-mounted television in the entryway to the gallery. View the web app

Created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A collaboration with FuzzyWobble and Dan Selden.
