MFA Design+Technology Thesis Show GIF Installation

In addition to traditional headshots, the 2013 thesis students from the Parsons MFA Design+Technology program created animated GIF portraits for the exhibition website. This installation was created for the exhibition gallery show and was displayed on a wall-mounted television in the entryway to the gallery.

Created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A collaboration with FuzzyWobble and Dan Selden.
